Frequently Asked

Who can participate in Techfest 2026?

Techfest is open to students from all colleges and universities worldwide. Professionals can attend as visitors. Individual and team registrations are available based on event type.

When and where is Techfest 2026?

Techfest 2026 will be held December 22–24, 2026 at IIT Bombay, Powai, Mumbai. Accommodation options are available near campus for outstation participants.

How do I register for competitions?

Visit techfest.org/register, create an account, and select competitions or workshops. Team registrations can be done from the team section after signing up.

Are workshops included in the festival pass?

Workshops require separate registration with individual fees. The general festival pass gives access to exhibitions, keynotes and Technoholix performances.

What is the total prize pool?

Techfest offers over ₹1 Crore in total prize money across all competitions. Individual events like Robowars and Hackathons carry substantial individual prizes.
